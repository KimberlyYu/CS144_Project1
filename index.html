My Favorite Games

Animal Crossing

Animal Crossing is a cozy life simulation game where you build your island paradise and make friends with cute animal villagers.
My favorite parts about the game:

  • being able to dress up my character
    • obtaining clothing designs from real-life players
  • decorating my island and home
    • building my own furniture and paying alpacas to customize it
  • chatting with villagers and listening to their jokes
  • cooking various foods and baking desserts
  • catching big fish and rare bugs

You can find more info about the game on the Animal Crossing website.

Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is a relaxing farming RPG where you grow crops, raise animals, and build relationships with villagers. One of my favorite parts of the game is the music. Dance of the Moonlight Jellies is one song I especially like.
